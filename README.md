## What is VioletPatch?

VioletPatch is a minimalist audio routing tool designed for creators who need quick and efficient audio management without the overhead of a full DAW.

Tired of opening Reaper, Logic Pro, or other heavy software just to route audio between devices? VioletPatch solves that problem. It's incredibly lightweight and does one thing really well: **route your audio**.
